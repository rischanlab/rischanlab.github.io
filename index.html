Rischan Mafrur

VIP in Data @CryptoDataWarehouse

I am a very curious person, especially when it comes to matters related to data. I am passionate about data and analytics, and I also love teaching. In 2023, I received a PhD degree from The University of Queensland (UQ), Australia. During my PhD, I joined the Data Science Research Group. My research interests include Data Exploration and Visualization. I also tutored several courses at UQ, including Advanced Database Systems (INFS3200), Database Principles (INFS7901), and Relational Database Systems (INFS2200). I earned my master’s degree in Computer Engineering from Chonnam National University, South Korea, and a bachelor’s degree in IT from Sunan Kalijaga State Islamic University, Indonesia.

In my free time, I often participate in Kaggle data science competitions, and I am currently a Kaggle competition expert. I also frequently undertake casual projects related to data science (analysis, machine learning modeling, etc.). I worked as a back-end software engineer at Kartoza Pty Ltd from 2015 to 2017. If you need assistance with data-related problems, please don’t hesitate to contact me via email.

Recently, my focus has been predominantly on cryptocurrency data.

In 2021, I started educating Indonesian millennials and Gen Z individuals about Blockchain, Cryptocurrency, Bitcoin, Decentralized Finance (DeFi), Decentralized apps (Dapps), and Non-fungible tokens (NFTs). In November 2022, I joined a 4-week bootcamp held by Covalent. Out of 1200 registered applicants, 250 were selected, and 125 completed the bootcamp. The bootcamp consisted of 4 modules and 4 challenges. Participants who completed all the bootcamp challenges received a reward in the form of money and became Covalent Web3 Data Alchemist fellows.

  • Award for Outstanding Content Creation: I received all Above and Beyond awards as an outstanding content creator and had the chance to share my learning experience during the bootcamp at its closing.

Feel free to reach out to me if you need help related to data and crypto.
